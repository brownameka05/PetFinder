ABOUT US

The mission of Pet Overflow is to improve the quality of life for all animals in Houston, especially those under the care of the City of Houston’s shelter & adoptions. Donations to Pet Overflow directly benefit animals sheltered at, or otherwise cared for by us through purchases of medical, kennel, sanitary, and other supplies and equipment. Pet Overflow‘s efforts are also focused on raising public awareness among all Houstonians about homeless and at-risk animals throughout our community.
